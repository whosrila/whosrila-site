Bio

WHOSRILA is a Jamaican reggae crossover artist, songwriter, creative director, and self-producing force whose sound moves freely between hip-hop, R&B, reggae, and beyond — rooted in culture, driven by instinct. With a background in video directing, WHOSRILA brings a full creative vision to every record, from concept to completion. Featured on playlists by New Reggae 2026: Fresh Export and the Deadly Reggae Playlist, the music speaks for itself. Independent. Intentional. Undeniable.
